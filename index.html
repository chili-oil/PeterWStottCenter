Peter W. Stott Center

More information on the Peter W. Stott Center

The Peter W. Stott Center is moe commonly known to Portland State University students as the Viking Pavillion. The building is located in the park blocks and is a staple building for athletes and coaches. The building is home to many basketball games that take place in the main court. The building includes a new weight training facility, locker rooms, an academic center, offices, a café and several student lounges, study spaces, and a collaborative spaces for OHSU’s Sports Medicine Center.
